Iconic Towers are four 38-storey skyscrapers, among the tallest in Panvel, showcasing breathtaking views and modern architecture. Located in the prime area of Ashte, Panvel, the project offers excellent connectivity to the Navi Mumbai International Airport, Mumbai-Pune Expressway, and Panvel Railway Station. The development boasts lush green spaces, with 70% open areas featuring landscaped gardens, jogging tracks, and a serene environment. Additionally, the township provides seamless convenience with high-street retail, restaurants, and essential services, making for a hassle-free lifestyle.
